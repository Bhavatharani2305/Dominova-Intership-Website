Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply?

Any college student from a technical or non-technical background looking to gain practical industry experience is welcome to apply.

Is it beginner friendly?

Absolutely! We start from the basics and gradually move to advanced concepts. Our mentors provide full doubt support throughout the journey.

What is the duration?

The duration is flexible based on student requirements. It can be 1 month, 2 months, or customized accordingly.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, upon successful completion of the program and projects, you will receive an MSME Registered Company internship completion certificate.
